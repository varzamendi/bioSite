About Eli

My subject for my bioSite project is my husband, Eli. Eli is a very kind and patient individual with numerous qualities. His passion is music and music production. He is currently producing an album for our friendâ€™s band the Cazadores.

He is a perfectionist; sometimes to a fault. He will work tirelessly until his feels what ever it is he is working on is just right. He never excepts good enough. I suppose that is what makes him a really good music producer.

For the sake of this project I will only focus on his passion with music as I feel it will produce the most content for the site.


Eli began learning music later on in life. In 2010 he moved to Seattle and studied audio engineering at Shoreline College. About a year later, he decided that he would rather learn the production side of music and learn how to use digital audio workstations to create and produce music.

By 2012, Eli moved to San Francisco where he attended Pyramind - SF Music Production School. There he crafted his skills more professionally. When he completed the program he earned several certifications in: Logic Pro, Pro Tools, Reason, and digital sound production.

In his free time, he works on learning new techniques and innovative ways to create and produce quality music. His DAW of choice is Logic Pro and Reason, however, he has used both Ableton and Pro Tools.

Eli is also the rhythm guitarist for the Cazadores. playing this role in the band has been a good experience for him as he is not someone who typically likes attention. He is usually happiest behind the scenes.
